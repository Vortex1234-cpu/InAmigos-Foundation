Who We Are

A Movement
Rooted in Compassion

Founded by Mr. Govind Shukla, InAmigos Foundation believes every person deserves dignity, opportunity, and belonging — regardless of their background.

✔ Section 8 Registered ✔ 80G & 12A Certified ✔ CSR-1 Registered ✔ NITI Aayog Registered ✔ ISO 9001:2015 Certified
Our Mission: To serve communities compassionately and create opportunities for every underprivileged person to grow with dignity and respect — across education, welfare, women's empowerment, environment, and livelihood.
"At InAmigos Foundation, we believe in the power of collective action. Every contribution is directed towards essential causes and lasting social change."
— Mr. Govind Shukla, Founder & CEO
